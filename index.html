Why Join Sistech

Sistech is a European non-profit founded in 2017. We empower refugee women through access to tech & digital jobs. Through weekly, online sessions with professionals, the Fellows enrich their practical knowledge, understand the local job market better, and design the next step in their career. More importantly, they join a community of displaced women and tech volunteers committed to accelerating their economic inclusion. The community empowers them with knowledge and tools, so Fellows can make their own career choices!
